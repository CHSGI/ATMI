360° of Excellence

In today’s world, it is extremely improbable to see a man who is successful in all the significant areas of life. You can see a successful businessman who is a complete failure on the family front or a dutiful scholar who is not disciplined financially.

The news is replete with stories of successful politicians who lack the requisite morality commensurate with their office. All these are the realities of our time that 360° of Excellence seeks to correct. This book will no doubt guide you on how to etch an enviable imprint in your career, business, relationship, marriage, and above all, God’s kingdom. Join me as we move from angle 90° to 360°!
